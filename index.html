Iroh was a retired Fire Nation General, a former Crown Prince of the nation, a Grand Lotus of the Order of the White Lotus, a firebending master, and a wise mentor to his nephew Zuko. Unlike many other individuals from the Fire Nation, Iroh was a wise, open-minded, kind and generous man. He appreciated and admired the balance of the four elements, and even incorporated aspects of the other elements into his own firebending techniques.
